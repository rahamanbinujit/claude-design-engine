# Book Spines / Shelfie Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## The Core Idea
Spine HEIGHT = data. The thickest book (AI Systems, 180px) has the most skills (18). The thinnest (Sales Pipeline, 65px) has the fewest (2). The viewer reads the data just by looking at the proportions.

## Palette (7 muted colors, no clashing)
- Navy `#2B4C7E`, Brick `#8B3A2A`, Forest `#1A5C3A`
- Purple `#5C4A8A`, Gold `#B8963E`, Teal `#3D6B6E`, Brown `#6B4A3A`

## Shelf
- Background: dark wood `#1C1916`
- Top/bottom edges: gradient + shadow for physical depth

## Spine Layout
- Horizontal strips, full width
- Title (serif, left-aligned) + Author/details (sans, right-aligned)
- Publisher mark: 4px vertical bar on left edge of each spine
- Ghost number (large, very low opacity) behind each spine

## Best For
- Skills/capability inventory posts
- "What's on my shelf" / reading list format
- Any content where proportional comparison adds meaning
