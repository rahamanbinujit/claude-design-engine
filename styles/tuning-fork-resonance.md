## Core Concept
The poster mimics an acoustic resonance analysis diagram. A tuning fork silhouette at left-center emits sound waves. Non-resonant frequencies (dim white) fade quickly. At 66 Hz, the waves turn RED and AMPLIFY instead of fading -- resonance. A frequency scale with amplitude bars on the right proves the spike visually.

## Metaphor
A tuning fork doesn't chase frequencies. It vibrates at its natural frequency, and the right things resonate. 66 skills tuned to the same fundamental. The fork IS the person -- fixed, intrinsic, unchanging. The resonance IS the output -- effortless, amplifying, infinite.

## Visual Structure
- **Canvas:** 1080x1350, #0A0A0A dark bg
- **Tuning fork:** Left-center, two prongs + stem in thin white lines (2.5px, 20% opacity), elegant instrument silhouette
- **Non-resonant waves:** 2-4 dim white wave pairs (0.025-0.05 opacity) fading quickly from prong tips
- **Resonant waves:** 5-7 RED ellipses radiating outward from fork, GROWING brighter/larger (30% down to 4% red), with glow filter on inner rings
- **Frequency scale:** Vertical ruler on right edge, tick marks at 10 Hz intervals, tiny amplitude bars. 66 Hz gets massive red bar (5-10x others)
- **Ghost "66":** Large (340px) at 4% red opacity, right side background texture
- **"66" number:** 160px at 20% red, right-center
- **Skill labels:** Decorative density text (7-8px mono, 8-12% white) scattered in clear dark space on both sides

## Typography
- **Line 1:** "They played" -- 44px, weight 300, 15% white (the crowd's dim attempt)
- **Line 2:** "every frequency." -- 68px, weight 700, 92% white (undeniable scale)
- **Line 3:** "I found resonance at 66." -- 42px italic Playfair Display red, with "66." at 54px bold Space Grotesk (the discovery)
- **Body:** Two paragraphs explaining the resonance metaphor
- **Data strip:** FREQ / MODE / AMPLITUDE / DAMPING / HARMONIC in 9px mono

## Color Palette
- Background: #0A0A0A
- White elements: 5-20% opacity (non-resonant, structural)
- Red elements: #C83C32 at 4-55% opacity (resonant waves, markers, accent text)
- Single red accent rule: resonance = the ONE color

## Best For
- "Finding your frequency" positioning
- Anti-hustle / alignment messaging
- Systems that compound through resonance, not force
- Physics meets personal brand
- Any post about natural talent meeting systematic effort

## Key Rules
- ALL text on clear dark background, never on wave lines or shapes
- Non-resonant waves FADE (smaller, dimmer). Resonant waves AMPLIFY (larger, brighter). This contrast IS the design.
- Frequency scale amplitude bars create the "scientific proof" -- one spike among flat responses
- Skill labels are DECORATIVE (density message), not communicative -- 7-8px is fine
- The fork is elegant and minimal, not chunky -- thin lines, instrument-grade precision
