# Brutalist Stack Columns

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


**Best for:** Systems-thinking, process-oriented, builder-mindset, "structure over chaos" posts

## Layout

- **Format:** 1080x1350, #0A0A0A background
- **Structure:** Two unequal vertical columns -- left 63%, right 37%
- **Divider:** 4px vertical bar at 8% white with tick marks every 10% height

## Left Column (63%)

- **Headline:** Massive stacked type, one word per line
- **Font:** Space Grotesk Bold, 92px, -4px tracking, line-height 0.92
- **Rhythm:** Alternate between full white, faded (30% white), and red accent words
- **Key word:** One word in #C83C32 (the core concept)
- **Label:** "COL A -- PRIMARY" in 9px Space Mono at 18% white

## Right Column (37%)

- **Body text:** Space Grotesk 22px at 38% white
- **Process steps:** Numbered list (01-05) in 9px Space Mono at 14% white -- barely visible structural texture
- **Stat:** 128px Space Grotesk Bold in #C83C32
- **Stat label:** 10px Space Mono at 25% white, uppercase
- **Stat context:** 15px Space Grotesk at 22% white
- **Secondary stats:** 36px bold white, 8px mono labels
- **Label:** "COL B -- SECONDARY" in 9px Space Mono at 18% white

## Signature Elements

1. **Red horizontal slash:** Full-width, 6px tall, 40% red opacity at 66.6% height -- cuts across both columns
2. **Column width annotations:** "680PX / 63%" and "396PX / 37%" in 7px mono at 18% red, top corners
3. **Horizontal grid lines:** At 20/40/60/80% height, 1px at 2.5% white
4. **Divider tick marks:** 12px wide, 1px, 6% white on the vertical divider
5. **Layout annotation:** Bottom-left, 8px mono at 12% white (e.g., "LAYOUT: BRUTALIST STACK / 1080X1350 / GRID: 12COL")

## Author Bar

- Bottom-right of right column
- 1px border-top at 7% white
- Name: 13px Space Grotesk Bold at 50% white, uppercase
- Title: 9px Space Mono at 18% white, uppercase

## Red Divider

- 48px wide, 3px tall, 40% red opacity
- Between secondary stats and author bar

## Design Principles

- The scale contrast between columns IS the design -- massive left vs. detailed right creates tension
- Faded words create typographic rhythm without losing readability (never go below 25% white)
- Exposed structural annotations (column labels, width specs, grid lines) sell the "blueprint" aesthetic
- The red slash is the one dramatic gesture -- everything else is disciplined structure
- Numbered process steps serve double duty: fill space AND add content value
