## Reference-Informed Design Decisions
1. **Palette from references**: Muted cream/amber/sepia from "colors of herbs used for remedies"
2. **Double-line border from references**: "Suggests something precious" (ZillionDesigns)
3. **Victorian typography from references**: Heavy serifs + flowing italic (18th-19th century)
4. **Distressing from references**: Foxing marks, paper grain = authenticity
5. **Full-canvas label from references**: No padding, the paper IS the design

## Palette
- **Paper:** Aged cream `#EDE4D3`
- **Ink:** Deep sepia `#2C2418`
- **Foxing:** Multiple radial-gradient warm spots at 6-12% opacity

## Typography
- **Brand:** Cinzel Decorative, 68px, weight 700 (Victorian display)
- **Body/Prescription:** IM Fell English, 15-17px, italic (period-authentic serif)
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond, 14-16px, weight 600, wide letter-spacing
- **Metadata:** Inconsolata, 8-10px, monospace
