# Warm Earth Editorial Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Background:** Warm cream `#F2EBE1`
- **Text:** Deep brown `#2C2418`
- **Accent:** Terracotta `#C45A3C`
- **Numbers/Highlight:** Warm gold `#D4A54A`
- **Secondary:** Sage green `#7A8B6F`

## Typography
- **Headlines:** DM Serif Display, 78px, regular + italic for accent. Letter-spacing -0.02em
- **Body:** Inter, 16-24px, weight 300, rgba(44,36,24,0.35-0.40)
- **Labels/Mono:** JetBrains Mono, 8-10px, letter-spacing 2-4px, uppercase
- **Key insight:** Serif italic on warm bg creates editorial warmth that sans-serif on dark bg can't

## Layout
- 1080x1350, cream bg
- Magazine masthead at top with thin bottom border
- Headline in upper-left 60%, photo in lower-right
- Stats stacked vertically on left with thin bottom borders
- Pull quote with terracotta left border
- Author bar at bottom with thin top border

## Visual Elements
- **Organic shape:** Blob via border-radius 60%/40%/50%, radial gradient, terracotta tint 10%
- **Sage circle:** Outline-only circle (1px, 12% sage), partially off-canvas
- **Paper texture:** SVG feTurbulence at 4% opacity, mix-blend-mode: multiply
- **Accent line:** 80px x 3px, terracotta at 50%

## Photo Treatment
- `filter: sepia(0.15) brightness(1.0) contrast(1.05) saturate(0.9)`
- Photos look MORE natural on light bg than dark
- Border-radius: 4px for soft editorial feel
- Caption below in mono, right-aligned

## Best For
- Reflective, philosophical posts
- Patience/slow-building/intentionality content
- Thought leadership that feels warm, not aggressive
- Personal stories, career reflections
- Any post where the tone is calm confidence, not urgent energy
