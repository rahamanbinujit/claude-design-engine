# Memphis Pop Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Background:** Warm cream `#FFF5E1`
- **Pink:** `#FF6B8A`
- **Teal:** `#00C9B7`
- **Yellow:** `#FFD93D`
- **Purple:** `#845EC2`
- **Coral:** `#FF8066`
- **Navy text:** `#1A1A2E`

## The Memphis Shapes
- **Circle:** Pink, 260px, bleeds off top-left
- **Semicircle:** Teal, right edge, flat bottom
- **Triangle:** Purple, CSS border-trick, bottom-right
- **Zigzag bar:** Yellow, full-width, clip-path polygon
- **Squiggle:** SVG quadratic bezier path, coral, 2.5px stroke
- **Confetti:** Dots (12-18px, various colors), crosses (+ at 28px, rotated), small squares (14-20px, rotated), outline circles

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Rubik, weight 900, uppercase. ALTERNATE SIZE + COLOR per line:
  - Line 1: 68px, navy (setup)
  - Line 2: 96px, pink (the break)
  - Line 3: 78px, navy (setup)
  - Line 4: 96px, teal (the payoff)
- **Body:** Rubik, 18px, weight 400, 50% navy
- **Tags:** Space Mono, 10px, weight 700, purple with purple bg pill
- **Stat pills:** Rubik, 32px, weight 900, white on colored pill (40px border-radius)
- **Quote:** Rubik, 22px, weight 300, 40% navy. Bold accent in weight 900 purple.

## Layout Rules
- Shapes can OVERLAP text -- this is Memphis, not Swiss grid
- Confetti scattered across entire canvas at various opacities
- One shape per corner/edge (don't cluster)
- Zigzag divider between content zones
- Stats as pill-shaped colored buttons (pink/teal/yellow)
- Footer: 4 small colored dots as palette signature

## Best For
- Light-hearted, fun posts
- "Breaking the mold" messaging
- Creative/playful positioning
- Posts about joy, energy, fun in work
- Pattern interrupts in the LinkedIn feed (this will STOP the scroll)
- Any content where the tone is "I'm serious but I don't take myself seriously"
