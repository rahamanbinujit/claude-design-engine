# Cassette / Mixtape Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Shell bg:** Warm dark `#1A1714`
- **J-card:** Cream `#F2E8D5`
- **Label:** Faded `#E8DCC8`
- **Tape brown:** `#3D2E1E`
- **Pen blue:** `#2B4C8C`
- **Pen red:** `#C0392B`
- **Pencil:** `#5A5248`

## Cassette Elements
- **Label:** Rounded rect with repeating-gradient lines, bordered
- **Hubs:** 60px circles with nested inner circles (border only)
- **Tape window:** Dark brown rect with inset box-shadow between hubs
- **J-card:** Cream bg with fold line (dashed border)
- **Spine text:** Centered on fold line, mono, very dim

## Typography
- **Label title:** Permanent Marker, 32px, blue
- **Side headers:** Permanent Marker, 22px, red
- **Track names:** Caveat, 20px, blue (red for starred tracks)
- **Track numbers:** Courier Prime, 12px, dim
- **Durations:** Courier Prime, 11px, dim
- **Annotations:** Caveat, 14px, pencil gray, rotated
- **Credits:** Caveat, 20px, blue/pencil

## Track Listing Format
```
A1  Video Editing (The First Skill)     4:32
A2  Motion Graphics                      3:48
A3  Marketing Analytics ★               5:15
```

## Best For
- Nostalgic/personal posts
- "My journey" as a playlist
- Skill timeline content
- "Curated for you" messaging
- Any post where the metaphor is personal curation + sharing
