# Botanical Illustration Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Paper bg:** Aged linen `#F6F3EC`
- **Text:** Deep forest `#2D3A2E`
- **Primary:** Sage green `#6B8F71`
- **Secondary:** Dried herb `#A4956B`
- **Accent:** Pressed flower rose `#C4727A`
- **Light:** Faded leaf `#D5CDBC`

## SVG Botanical Illustration
- **Stem:** Quadratic bezier curves (Q control points), 1-1.5px stroke, 25% sage
- **Leaves:** Paired bezier paths (stroke + 3% fill), dashed vein centerlines
- **Flower bud:** 3 concentric circles (different radii + opacities) + 5 petal suggestion paths
- **Roots:** 3 thin diverging lines from stem base, 8-12% herb color
- **Annotations:** Dashed lines from each part to margin labels ("Fig. A — Corolla")
- **Scale bar:** Two tick marks + line + "10 cm" label

## Typography
- **Latin name:** Lora, 14px, italic, 30% forest (top-left, scientific classification)
- **Headline:** Lora, 52px, weight 400. Green italic on accent word.
- **Body:** Karla, 16px, weight 300, 40% forest
- **Classification labels:** Karla, 10px, weight 500, 2px letter-spacing, 25% forest
- **Classification values:** Lora, 14px. Green for highlights, rose for special marks.
- **Collection note:** Lora, 13px, italic, 30% forest
- **Annotations:** Fira Code, 8px, 15% forest
