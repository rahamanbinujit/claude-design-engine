# Polaroid Photo Wall Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## The Mood
This is the WARMEST, most PERSONAL design. Not a metaphor — real moments on a real board. Use when authenticity > authority.

## Cork Surface
- Background: `#C4A882`
- Multi-layer radial gradients for tonal variation
- Noise grain via SVG at 6% multiply

## Polaroid Frame
```css
.polaroid {
  background: #FAFAF8;
  padding: 12px 12px 48px 12px; /* thick bottom = caption area */
  box-shadow: 2px 3px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.15);
}
```
- Caption: Caveat cursive, 16px, centered in bottom margin

## Layout Rules
- 5 photos at varied positions and rotations (-5° to +5°)
- Slight overlaps between 1-2 photos for depth
- Tape strips (semi-transparent, matching photo rotation)
- Colored pushpins (red, blue, yellow, green)
- Handwritten notes on cork between photos
- Title card (text-only Polaroid) at bottom

## Best For
- Personal/authentic posts ("who I am" not "what I built")
- Journey retrospectives with real photos
- Mood board / vision board format
- Any post where warmth and personality > polish and authority
