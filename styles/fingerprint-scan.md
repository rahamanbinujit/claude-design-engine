# Fingerprint Scan

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


Biometric fingerprint scan aesthetic. The system's identity is encoded in its fingerprint -- the stat IS the identity core, embedded within organic ridge patterns. A scanner has verified: MATCH FOUND.

## Specs

- **Canvas:** 1080x1350, #0A0A0A background
- **Fingerprint:** 30+ SVG concentric organic curves (ellipses + bezier paths). NOT perfect circles -- slightly irregular, like real ridge patterns. Opacity: 6% (outer) to 12% (inner core). Stroke: 1.0px (outer) to 1.5px (core). Spacing tightens toward center (whorl pattern). Upper arches + lower arches create a gap at center where stat sits.
- **Minutiae points:** 7 red match points (3-3.5px circles, 25-40% red) clustered at core with 8-10px glow halos. 10 white ridge endings (2px, 8-10% white) on outer ridges.
- **Scan line:** 2px horizontal, gradient red (peak at center, fades to edges). 62px vertical glow band (10% red). Positioned above or through the stat zone.
- **Scanning frame:** 4 L-brackets (40x40px, 1.5px, 30% red) framing the fingerprint zone at its four corners.
- **Crosshair:** 1px lines at 12% red at center, short (60px each direction).
- **Center glow:** Radial gradient (500x400px, 12% red peak) behind fingerprint center.
- **Stat:** 200-220px bold #C83C32 at fingerprint center with 80px/160px red glow. "Skills Identified" mono label below.
- **MATCH FOUND:** Red dot (8px) with glow + "MATCH FOUND" in 11px Space Mono, 85% red, letter-spacing 3px. Positioned right of scan zone. "CONFIDENCE: 99.7%" below in 9px mono 22% white.

## Typography

- **Tag:** Red dot + "Identity" in 10px Space Mono, 25% white, letter-spacing 2px
- **Headline line 1:** "Every system has" -- 48px, weight-300, 14% white. The setup whisper.
- **Headline line 2:** "a signature." -- 82px, weight-700, 93% white. "signature." in #C83C32 with 50px/100px red glow. The confident claim.
- **Body:** 22px, weight-300, 35% white. Italic emphasis line at 55% white.
- **Stats row:** 38px bold numbers + 10px mono labels
- **Identity bar:** 13px name, 9px mono role, red bar accent
- **Edge metadata:** 7px mono, 8% white, rotated along left/right edges
- **Data panel:** 8px mono, 16% white, right-aligned. SCAN/MATCH/ID/ACCESS fields.

## Text Placement Logic

1. Headline at TOP-LEFT = narrative flow, the claim before the proof
2. "signature." in RED = identity IS the concept, the color marks the key word
3. "66" at CENTER of ridges = the identity core, every ridge wraps around this number
4. "MATCH FOUND" at SCAN LINE HEIGHT = the system's verdict at the moment of recognition
5. Body at BOTTOM-THIRD = grounding/conclusion, the editorial insight
6. Data panel at BOTTOM-RIGHT = whispered system specs for close readers

## Ghost element

- Faded "ID" at 480-500px, 1.8% opacity, centered behind fingerprint
