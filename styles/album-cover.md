# Album Cover Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Background:** Near-black `#111111`
- **Text:** Off-white `#E8E4DF`
- **Accent:** Electric orange `#E8622C`
- **Secondary:** Pale lavender `#B8A9C9`
- **Tint:** Warm gray `#3A3632`

## Typography
- **Album title:** Bebas Neue, 130px, uppercase, 8px letter-spacing. One word white, one word orange.
- **Artist name:** Syne, 13px, weight 700, 8px letter-spacing, uppercase, 35% opacity
- **Subtitle:** Syne, 16px, weight 400, lavender at 45%, 2px letter-spacing
- **Tracklist:** Courier Prime, 9px, 18% opacity. Highlighted tracks in orange at 40%.
- **Label info:** Courier Prime, 8px, 12% opacity, 2px letter-spacing

## Layout
- 1080x1350 portrait
- Artist name at top (left-aligned) with release info (right-aligned)
- Photo center with 80px side margins, 120px top margin, 740px tall
- Album title overlapping photo bottom edge (centered, two lines stacked)
- Subtitle centered below title
- Tracklist in two columns near bottom
- Label info + barcode at very bottom

## Photo Treatment
- `filter: grayscale(1) contrast(1.4) brightness(0.85)` -- dramatic B&W
- Subtle color tint overlay via mix-blend-mode: color (orange from top-left, lavender from bottom-right)
- Bottom fade: only bottom 25% of photo fades to bg
- Top fade: subtle, 100px, 40% opacity
- KEY INSIGHT: B&W high-contrast makes silhouette/landscape photos cinematic

## Visual Elements
- **Barcode:** 12 thin bars (2px wide) at varying heights, 10% opacity
- **Lavender glow:** 300px radial gradient at 4% opacity, bottom-right atmosphere
- **Divider:** 0.5px line at 6% above tracklist
