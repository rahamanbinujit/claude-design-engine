## Palette
- **Background:** Warm off-white `#F5F0E8`
- **Black:** `#1A1A1A`
- **Red:** `#D4382C` (circle)
- **Yellow:** `#F2C12E` (triangle)
- **Blue:** `#2656A8` (square)
- **Light gray:** `#D8D3CA` (outlines)

## The Three Shapes (Kandinsky's Theory)
Each shape is a PRIMARY ELEMENT that carries data:
- **Red Circle** (440px, bleeds off canvas): The system. Organic, complete, infinite.
- **Blue Square** (180px): Holds the primary stat. Stable, structural, rational.
- **Yellow Triangle** (CSS border-trick): Holds secondary stat. Dynamic, pointing, energetic.

## Typography
- **Headline:** DM Sans, 88px, weight 900, uppercase, -0.03em. Geometric, Futura-adjacent.
- **Red accent word:** Same size, `#D4382C`
- **Subline:** DM Sans, 16px, weight 300, 45% black
- **Stat on blue:** DM Sans, 72px, weight 900, off-white
- **Secondary stats:** DM Sans, 42px, weight 900 + Space Mono 9px labels
- **Manifesto:** DM Sans, 20px, weight 300/900 contrast
- **Label:** DM Sans, 10px, weight 900, 6px letter-spacing

## Structural Elements
- **Black bar:** Full or partial width, 12px height. Creates horizon/division.
- **Vertical line:** 2px, spans ~500px. Creates intersection with bar.
- **Diagonal:** 2px, ~280px, 15% opacity, rotated. Adds tension.
- **Gray circle outline:** 1.5px stroke, no fill. Depth/echo of red circle.
- **Small black circle:** 40px, solid. Punctuation mark.

## Layout Rules
- Shapes overlap text zones -- text weaves AROUND geometry
- Each shape occupies a different zone (top-right, mid-right, bottom-left)
- Structural lines connect zones visually
- Stats embedded IN shapes, not next to them
- Footer: three small dots (red/yellow/blue) = palette signature
