# Newspaper Front Page Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Newsprint:** `#F5F2EB`
- **Ink:** `#1A1A1A`
- **Rules:** rgba(26,26,26,0.08-0.12)

## Hierarchy (from research)
1. Masthead: Playfair Display, 80px, weight 900
2. Main headline: Playfair Display, 52px, weight 800
3. Deck: Source Serif 4, 18px, weight 300
4. Body: Source Serif 4, 14px, two columns with divider
5. Secondary headlines: Playfair Display, 20px, weight 700
6. Metadata: Inconsolata, 8-11px

## Key Elements
- Drop cap (56px Playfair) on first paragraph
- Column divider (0.5px vertical line)
- Photo with italic caption + mono credit
- 3-column secondary stories below 2px rule
- Edition info in masthead (VOL, date, price)

## Best For
- "Breaking news" style announcements
- Data/achievement reveals with journalistic authority
- Story-format content
