# Stained Glass Rose Window Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Concept
Gothic cathedral rose window treating skills/systems as individual glass panels held together by one framework (the person). The lead came tracery IS the person -- the invisible structure that makes the beauty possible.

## Structure (SVG, 940px viewBox)
1. **Ring 5 (outer):** 24 lancet panels with pointed arch Q-curve tips, r=350 to r=435
2. **Ring 4 (petals):** 12 Gothic pointed arch petals (cubic bezier), r=195 to r=345, with subdivision + cross bars
3. **Ring 3 (roundels):** 12 single circles (r=18) at r=165, between petal bases
4. **Ring 2 (rosette):** 6 round lobes (r=40) at r=82, alternating crimson/amber. 6 gold roundels (r=14) between.
5. **Ring 1 (center):** Crimson r=40, amber r=22, gold dot r=8, "66" text overlay.
6. **Tracery:** Concentric came circles, 12+24+6 radial lines, gold dots at intersections.

## Glass Effect
Each panel uses radialGradient: bright center (45%, 35% offset) fading to deep dark edges. Simulates light passing through colored glass.

## Layout (1080x1350)
