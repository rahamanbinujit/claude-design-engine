# Japanese Minimal Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Philosophy
- **Ma** (間): The space between things is not empty -- it's the most important element
- **Wabi-sabi**: Imperfection is beauty. Ink splatters, incomplete circles, faded edges
- 60%+ of the canvas should be empty space
- Every element must EARN its place. If you can remove it without losing meaning, remove it

## Layout
- 1080x1350 portrait, #0A0A0A background
- Headline in upper-left third (not centered -- asymmetry is intentional)
- Single vertical red brushstroke on right third (4px wide, 520px tall)
- Thin horizon line at ~56% height (0.5px, gradient-faded)
- Ghost stat below horizon (120px, 12% red opacity)
- Author bar at very bottom

## Visual Elements
- **Brushstroke**: 4px wide, linear-gradient (transparent -> #C83C32 -> transparent). 55% opacity. Blur glow behind (16px, 8px blur)
- **Ink splatters**: 2-5px dots near brushstroke base, rgba(200,60,50,0.15), varying opacity
- **Enso circle**: 280px, 1px border, 2.5% white, clip-path to make it incomplete
- **Horizon line**: 0.5px, gradient-faded from transparent at edges to 6% white at center

## Typography
- Whisper: 22px / weight-300 / 18% white (the setup line)
- Headline: 72px / weight-700 / white (the declaration)
- Accent: 72px / weight-700 / italic / #C83C32 (the payoff)
- Ghost stat: 120px / weight-700 / 12% #C83C32 (deliberately faint)
- Body: 17px / weight-300 / 28% white (minimal, one thought)

## Best For
- Philosophical/reflective posts
- "Less is more" messaging
- Intentionality, focus, discipline content
- Systems thinking where the emphasis is on what you DON'T do
- Any post where the message is about restraint, clarity, signal vs noise
