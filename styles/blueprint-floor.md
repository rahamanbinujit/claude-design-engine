# Blueprint Floor Plan Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## The Golden Rule (from research)
Consistent stroke weight across ALL elements. Any variation breaks the blueprint illusion.

## Palette
- **Background:** Cyanotype blue `#0A2342`
- **Lines:** Cyan-white `rgba(140,200,255,...)` at varying opacities
- **Walls:** 0.30 opacity, 2px (structural) / 1.5px (interior)
- **Details:** 0.10-0.15 opacity, 0.5-1px

## Best For
- Systems architecture posts
- "Here's how it's built" breakdowns
- Department/skill mapping content
