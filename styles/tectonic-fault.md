# Tectonic Fault — Style Reference

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Concept
Tectonic plate convergence poster. Two geological plates (creative skills vs technical skills) collide at a diagonal fault line. The convergence zone is the epicenter where "66" sits. Strata layers carry embedded skill labels. Pressure arrows show convergent force. Seismic rings propagate from the epicenter.

## When to Use
- Posts about combining different skill domains
- Convergence / collision / fusion metaphors
- "Pressure creates diamonds" type messaging
- Creative + technical intersection content
- Depth vs surface messaging

## Visual Elements

### Background
- `#0A0A0A` solid
- Faded "66" at 600px, 1.8% white, centered

### Fault Line (hero element)
- Diagonal jagged polyline from upper-left to lower-right
- 45% red, 2.5px stroke with 12% red glow line (8px, filtered)
- 2 parallel secondary faults: dashed, 10% white

### Strata Layers
- 6 per plate (12 total), angled to follow plate tilt
- Left plate tilts down-right, right plate tilts up-right
- 5.5-8% white, 1px stroke
- Offset vertically across fault = displacement evidence

### Skill Labels
- 8px Space Mono, 3px letter-spacing, 13-18% white
- Rotated to follow strata angle
- Left plate: MARKETING, CONTENT, STRATEGY, DESIGN, VIDEO, WRITING
- Right plate: AI SYSTEMS, ANALYTICS, AUTOMATION, GROWTH, SALES, BRAND

### Pressure Arrows
- 3 per side, pushing toward center
- 15-18% white, 1.2px stroke with arrowhead polygons

### Seismic Rings
- 4 concentric: inner solid 20% red 1.5px, 3 dashed (14%, 9%, 6% red)
- 2 outer P-wave rings: 5% and 3.5% white

### Stat
- "66" at 240px bold #C83C32 at convergence center
- Triple red glow (80px and 160px radial)
- "Skills Converging" label: 11px mono, 6px letter-spacing, 35% white

### Red Glow
- Radial gradient ellipse at convergence: 18% center, 320x240px

## Typography

### Headline
- Line 1: 46px Space Grotesk weight-300, 16% white = surface observation (fragile, lightweight)
- Line 2: 52px Space Grotesk weight-700, 93% white = underground truth (conviction, force)
- Accent word: italic #C83C32 with 40px/15px glow = the payoff

### Body
- Dim: 17px weight-400, 25% white = everyone else (passive, eroding)
- Bright: 17px weight-400 italic, 58% white = the builder's claim (active, permanent)
- Red rule divider: 55px, 1px, 25% red

### Stats Row
- Numbers: 36px weight-700, 88% white
- Labels: 8px mono, 2px letter-spacing, 22% white
- Deltas: 7px mono, 45% red (geology-themed)

### Instrument Panel
- Right-aligned, 7px mono, 18% white / 50% red values
- SRC, TYPE, MAG, DEPTH, PLATES, CLASS

## Text Placement Logic
1. **Tag** top-left = the scientific instrument frame
2. **Headline line 1** (weight-300) = surface-level observation, fragile, what everyone sees
3. **Headline line 2** (weight-700) = the underground reality, where the real work happens
4. **Accent word** in italic red = the exact point of maximum pressure
5. **Strata with skill labels** = skills embedded in geological record, not listed but deposited
6. **Pressure arrows** = active convergence, skills colliding not just coexisting
7. **"66" at convergence center** = the epicenter, where plates meet, magnitude 6.6
8. **Body at bottom-third** = grounded conclusion past the zone of destruction
