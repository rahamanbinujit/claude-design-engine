# Morse Code Rhythm

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


**Category:** Data/Communication Aesthetic
**Quality Score:** 4/5 — KEEP

## When to Use
- "Hidden message" posts, builder-in-silence themes
- Communication-about-communication meta-posts
- Any content where the message IS the medium
- Secret knowledge, coded wisdom, insider themes
- Posts about systems working quietly in the background

## Canvas
- 1080x1350, `#0A0A0A` background
- SVG noise texture at 2.5% opacity

## Morse Code Band (Key Element)
- Positioned above headline, between two 1px horizontal lines at 5% white
- Dots: 8px red circles (`#C83C32`), 0.9 opacity
- Dashes: 24x5px red rectangles (`#C83C32`), 0.9 opacity, 2px border-radius
- 6px gap between elements within a letter
- 8px horizontal padding per letter group
- Vertical separator lines between letter groups: 1px at 6% white
- Encode the actual headline text in morse for authenticity
- "DECODE:" label above band: 10px Space Mono, 60% red, 3px letter-spacing

## Transmission Metadata (Top)
- "// INCOMING TRANSMISSION" — 9px Space Mono, 50% red, 2.5px letter-spacing
- "FREQ: 7.040 MHz / WPM: 25 / SIGNAL: STRONG" — 8px Space Mono, 20% white

## Signal Strength Indicator (Top-Right)
- 5 vertical bars, 4px wide, ascending heights (7/12/18/25/33px)
- Color: 45% red, opacity fading on taller bars (0.9 to 0.35)

## Headline
- 78px Space Grotesk Bold, -3.5px tracking, 0.93 line-height
- Key action words in full white
- Connector/preposition words dimmed at 18% white
- 1-2 accent words in `#C83C32`
- Line breaks chosen for emphasis (strongest word on its own line)

## Body Text
- 23px Space Grotesk Regular, 32% white, 1.55 line-height, 560px max-width

## Waveform Bridge (Middle Space)
- SVG polyline filling space between body and stat section
- 1.2px stroke at 12% red
- Mostly flat baseline with micro-tremors (2-3px random variations)
- 3 periodic signal spikes (amplitude 13-17px) suggesting ongoing transmission
- Creates visual interest without competing with content

## Stat Morse Band
- Smaller than headline band: dots 7px at 0.7 opacity, dashes 20x4px
- Red accent line 160px at 20% red above morse pattern
- "// DECODED SIGNAL" label: 9px Space Mono, 50% red

## Stat Number
- 168px Space Grotesk Bold, `#C83C32`, 0.82 line-height, -6px tracking
- "SKILLS AUTOMATED" (or context label): 11px Space Mono, 22% white, 3px letter-spacing

## Secondary Stats
- 38px Space Grotesk SemiBold, 45% white
- Labels: 8px Space Mono, 16% white, 2px letter-spacing
- 56px gap between stat groups

## Transmission Line (Separator)
- 1px height, linear gradient from 30% red (left) to 5% white (35%) to transparent

## Author Bar
- Name: 14px Space Grotesk Medium, 38% white
- Title: 8px Space Mono, 16% white, "SENIOR MARKETING EXECUTIVE"
- Right side: "END TRANSMISSION / MSG-XXXX / CLEARTEXT" in 7px Space Mono, 12% white

## Design Rules
- Red used ONLY for: morse dots/dashes, accent headline words, stat number, transmission labels, signal bars
- No drop shadows, no gradients on elements (except transmission line separator)
- All structural labels in Space Mono
- The morse band is the signature element — it must be prominent enough to read as a deliberate pattern
- Waveform is subtle texture, never dominant
