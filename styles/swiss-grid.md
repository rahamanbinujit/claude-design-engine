# Swiss Grid Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Philosophy
- The grid is not hidden infrastructure -- it IS the design
- Every element must snap to a grid intersection
- One geometric shape (circle, rectangle) as the dominant visual, often bleeding off the canvas edge
- Precision, order, clarity. No decoration. Function IS beauty.

## Layout
- 1080x1350 portrait, #0A0A0A background
- 6-column grid (72px margins, 24px gutters, ~136px columns)
- 8-row grid (60px margins, 20px gutters, ~136px rows)
- Visible grid lines at 0.5px, 3% white opacity
- Column labels at top (COL 1-6) as meta-reference, 7px mono, 4% white

## Visual Elements
- **Red circle**: 480px diameter, bleeding off top-right, radial gradient fill (14% center -> 6% -> transparent), 1.5px outline ring at 12% red
- **Stats bar**: Full-width, 5 cells with 1px dividers, border-top and border-bottom at 6% white
- **Pull quote**: Left-aligned, red 2px left border at 25% opacity

## Typography
- Tag: 9px Space Mono, 4px letter-spacing, 12% white
- Category: 11px Space Mono, 3px letter-spacing, #C83C32 at 60%
- Headline: 86px Space Grotesk, weight-700, -0.04em, line-height 0.92
- Red accent: Same size, #C83C32 (not italic -- Swiss style is strict, no italics)
- Subheadline: 20px, weight-300, 35% white
- Stats: 42px weight-700 (red for primary, white for secondary)
- Stat labels: 8px Space Mono, 3px letter-spacing, 20% white
