# Street Art Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Palette
- **Background:** Concrete gray `#2A2A28`
- **Primary accent:** Neon yellow-green `#CDFC41`
- **Secondary accent:** Hot pink `#FF3366`
- **Text:** Off-white `#EDEDED`
- **Shadow:** Deep charcoal `#1A1A18`

## Typography
- **Line 1:** Dela Gothic One, 110px, white, rotate(-2deg). Heavy, blocky.
- **Line 2:** Archivo Black, 148px, neon, rotate(1deg). Massive, loud.
- **Line 3:** Dela Gothic One, 80px, pink, rotate(-1.5deg). Accent color punch.
- **Subtext:** Overpass Mono, 18px, crossed-out in 15% white + bold neon response below
- **Stats:** Archivo Black, 64px (neon/white/pink variants)
- **Stat labels:** Overpass Mono, 9px, 3px letter-spacing, 20% white. ADD ATTITUDE to labels.
- **Tags:** Overpass Mono, 10px, 700 weight, bordered box

## Key Technique: Each Headline Line is Different
Different font + size + color + rotation on each line = authentic street art chaos. NOT random -- each choice is intentional:
- Line 1: setup (white, slightly left-tilted, standard weight)
- Line 2: the SHOUT (neon, slightly right-tilted, oversized, glow)
- Line 3: the punctuation (pink, left-tilted, smaller = the conclusion)

## Street Art Elements
- **Paint drips:** Thin vertical bars (3-5px wide, 35-80px tall) below headline, neon at 12% opacity
- **Splatter dots:** 3-8px circles scattered near headline, neon/pink at 8-18% opacity
- **Concrete texture:** Overlapping radial gradients at varied warmths + SVG noise at 25% overlay
- **Wheat-paste photo:** transform: rotate(1-2deg), 6px border at 8% white, tape strips
- **Tape strips:** 60-70px x 12-14px divs at low opacity, matching photo rotation
- **Stencil tag:** Mono text with 1px border, like a spray-painted stencil
- **X marks:** Graffiti tally counting, faint, atmospheric
- **Atmospheric glows:** Neon + pink radial gradients at 2.5-3% behind content zones

## Best For
- Rebellious/contrarian posts ("I didn't ask permission")
- Anti-corporate messaging
- Bold statement posts with maximum attitude
- Young energy, hustle culture content
- Any post where the tone is raw, unapologetic, in-your-face
