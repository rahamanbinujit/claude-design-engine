# Brute Stack

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## Concept
Pure typographic brutalism. No decoration, no metadata strips, no speed lines, no corner brackets. The entire poster is stacked uppercase type at crushing density, with an alternating dim/bright opacity rhythm that creates a redacted/visible pattern. The stat number punches through the stack as the only disruption. Maximum typographic impact, minimum decoration.

## When to Use
- Bold declaration posts
- Contrarian takes ("they said X can't Y")
- Capability statements
- Any content where the metaphor is "raw truth, no decoration"
- Posts that need to feel blunt, honest, zero-pretense

## Layout (1080x1350)
- Background: #0A0A0A
- Canvas padding: 48px all sides

### The Stack (top: 70px)
- 5-7 headline words, each on its own line
- Font: Space Grotesk, 126px, bold, uppercase
- Letter-spacing: -4px
- Line-height: 0.92 (crushing density)
- Words fill nearly the full width

### Dim/Bright Rhythm
- Bright words: 93% white (the message that "survives")
- Dim words: 6% white (context, feels redacted)
- One word in #C83C32 (the key word, usually the subject)
- Pattern creates visual redaction -- eye reads only the bright/red words

### Stat Breakthrough
- Font: Space Grotesk, 280px, bold, #C83C32
- Letter-spacing: -10px, line-height: 0.8
- Positioned to overlap 1-2 lines of the stack
- Text-shadow knockout: 60px at 95% dark, 120px at 70% dark (so stat dominates overlap zone)
- Mono tag below: "x066" format, 11px Space Mono, red 40%, letter-spacing 5px

### Body (bottom: 155px)
- Space Grotesk, 22px, weight-300, 28% white
- 1-2 lines max. No em dashes.

### Author Bar (bottom: 48px)
- 1px border-top at 6% white
- Name: 13px bold, 50% white
- Title: 9px Space Mono, 20% white, uppercase, letter-spacing 2px

### Noise
- SVG fractalNoise, 2.5% opacity

## What Makes It Work
- The crushing density of stacked uppercase fills the poster with raw presence
- The dim/bright alternation creates meaning through visibility -- the eye naturally reads only the bright words
- Zero decoration means the typography IS the design
- The red stat breaking through the stack is impossible to ignore because it's the only element that disrupts the vertical rhythm
- The absence of metadata, brackets, and ornament feels intentionally stripped -- brutalist honesty
