# Film Credits Style

> Part of the [Claude Design Engine](https://github.com/rahamanujit/claude-design-engine) style library. Adapt colors and fonts to your `brand.config.json`.


## The Rules (from research)
- ONE font family only (Cormorant Garamond)
- CENTERED always — no exceptions
- WHITE on BLACK — no other colors
- Size descends with importance
- Generous spacing between each credit block

## 5-Tier Hierarchy
1. Film title: 52px, weight 600, 90% white, 6px letter-spacing
2. Top-billed (director/star): 24px, weight 500, 75% white
3. Department heads: 18px, weight 400, 55% white
4. Crew/additional: 14px, weight 300, 35% white
5. Copyright/legal: 10px, weight 300, 10% white

## Role Labels
- 10-12px, weight 300, 4-5px letter-spacing, uppercase, 18-25% white
- Always ABOVE the name, never beside it

## Best For
- Dramatic career/achievement announcements
- "The credits roll" retrospective posts
- One-person-did-everything messaging
- Any post where cinematic gravitas adds weight
