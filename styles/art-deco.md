## Typography
- **Title:** Poiret One, 108px, uppercase, 6px letter-spacing. THE Art Deco font.
- **Big number:** Poiret One, 200px, gold. Thin geometric strokes = crafted elegance.
- **"The":** Raleway, 20px, weight 100, 18px letter-spacing, uppercase, 30% cream
- **Subtitle:** Raleway, 16px, weight 300, 10px letter-spacing, gold at 45%
- **Top label:** Raleway, 11px, weight 200, 14px letter-spacing, gold at 50%
- **Secondary stats:** Poiret One, 52px, cream. USE ROMAN NUMERALS (II, V, X).
- **Stat labels:** Cutive Mono, 9px, 3px letter-spacing, gold at 30%
- **Quote:** Raleway, 17px, weight 200, italic, cream at 30%. Bold words at weight 700, 55%.
- **Credits:** Raleway, 9px, weight 200, 8px letter-spacing, gold at 25%
