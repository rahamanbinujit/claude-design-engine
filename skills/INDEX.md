## How to Use These Skills

These skills are **automatically applied** by the design engine during every design task. You don't need to read them before designing — the system already knows them.

**Read them when:**
- You want to understand WHY the system makes certain design choices
- You want to manually improve a specific aspect of a design
- You're learning design and want to develop your own eye
- You want to give more specific feedback ("make the hierarchy more extreme")
